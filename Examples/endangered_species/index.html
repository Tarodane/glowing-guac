Elephants for Asia is a non-profit organization tasked with helping the declining Asian elephant population. The Asian elephant population has decreased by an estimated 60% over the last century. What's we're out to do is create boundaries between humans that hunt elephants for ivory and create habitats more suitable for elephant livelyhood.
