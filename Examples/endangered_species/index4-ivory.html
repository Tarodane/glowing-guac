Ivory

Ivory is most simply the tusk of elephants. The main reason that elephants are endangered is because of the Ivory trade. Elephant tusk is highly coveted because of its pure color and its ability to be carved. Carved pieces of ivory are at a high price mainly because of its rarity, ethical feel, and its looks.
